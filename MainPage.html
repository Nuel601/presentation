Available Services

Barangay Clearance

Official certification that you are a resident of Barangay 118 and have no pending barangay cases. Required for employment, business permits, and other government transactions.

Certificate of Residency

Proof of residence in Barangay 118. Required for school enrollment, scholarship applications, voter registration, and other documents requiring proof of address.

Certificate of Indigency

Certification that you belong to the low-income sector. Required for social welfare assistance, medical discounts, educational subsidies, and other government assistance programs.
